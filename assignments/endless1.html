Endless Mountains

The Endless Mountains are a chain of mountains in the Sleepless Wounderland. The Endless Mountains region includes Snow City, Heavenly City, Artificial Wonderland, and Silent Valley. The highest peak in the region is the North Knob of Endless Mountain at 2693 feet (821 meters).
